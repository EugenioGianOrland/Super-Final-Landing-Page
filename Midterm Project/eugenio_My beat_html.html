OPM

           It exemplifies Filipino culture. OPM is the Filipino people's beating heart. It appeals to the Filipino people's hearts and brains, and it demonstrates how brilliant Filipinos are in the music business. OPM is unique in that it was created by us and symbolizes us, the Filipinos.

Zack Tabudlo

           Zack Tabudlo is a Filipino singer-songwriter, musician and producer. He was first seen in The Voice Kids season 1 as a member of Team Bamboo. He later began his musical career in 2018 before he rose to fame in 2020 when he joined MCA Music/Universal through sublabel Island Records Philippines.




Arthur Nery

           Born and raised in Cagayan De Oro, Arthur Nery was always into music since he was young. In fact, music actually runs in the family as his parents are singers. He credits them for inspiring him to make music.

BLACKPINK

           Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa.





TWICE

           Twice is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed under the television program Sixteen and debuted on October 20, 2015, with the extended play The Story Begins.
